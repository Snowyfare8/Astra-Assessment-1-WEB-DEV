Reviews

This TV show is not perfect. In fact, depending on what you value in your television, you could even call The Expanse "bad." No, The Expanse is very good. Often it's good in spite of its flaws. Sometimes it's enhanced by those flaws. - Mark Serrels, Tomatometer

Epic, whip-smart and addictive, Amazon's near-future sci-fi series marries politics and space battles in the story of a future when we populate the solar system but remain culturally divided. - Kelly Lawler, USA Today

The best sci-fi show in years, based on its combination of interstellar politics, world-building, complex characters, and sleek overall look (that hasn't always been a given for televised genre, kids). - Nathan Mattise, Ars Technica
